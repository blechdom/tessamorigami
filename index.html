FIELD NOTES

A sheet can be a score.

The moving point reads the perimeter at constant speed. Height changes pitch, edge length controls time, and sharp turns strike harder. Repeat the seed and the same motif becomes a canon.

Fold lifts the sheet into depth. Beyond adds a second, displaced solid and connecting edges: the projected shadow of extrusion through a fourth axis. The pale moving section is the part intersecting our current three-dimensional slice.

This prototype uses the genuine tactile-js isohedral tiling engine under its BSD 3-Clause license. All sound is synthesized live in the browser.
